Frequently Asked Questions

What is NATA? ▼

NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture) is a national-level entrance exam conducted by the Council of Architecture (CoA) for admission to undergraduate B.Arch programs across India. It tests drawing, aesthetic sensitivity, and mathematical aptitude.

Who is eligible for NATA? ▼

Candidates who have passed or are appearing in 12th Standard with Mathematics as a compulsory subject are eligible. Students with a 10+3 Diploma (with Mathematics) are also eligible to appear for NATA.

When should I start NATA preparation? ▼

Ideally, start preparing from Class 11. However, even a focused 3–6 month preparation can yield excellent results. GKS NATA Academy offers both long-term foundation courses and short-term crash courses to suit your timeline.

What is the NATA passing score? ▼

The qualifying score for NATA is 70 out of 200. However, to secure admission to top colleges, students typically need scores between 120–160+. The score is valid for one year from the date of result declaration.

Does GKS offer online coaching? ▼

Yes! GKS NATA Academy offers live online coaching for students outside Thoothukudi. Our online programme includes live classes, recorded sessions, downloadable study materials, online mock tests and personalised drawing feedback.
